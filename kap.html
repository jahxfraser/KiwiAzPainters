Auckland's Trusted Painting Professionals

Kiwi Az Painters is a family owned and operated painting company based in Auckland Central. For over 15 years, we've been transforming homes and businesses across the Auckland region with our quality workmanship and attention to detail.

We take pride in being true professionals who turn up on time, respect your property, and deliver a finish that lasts. Whether it's a small touch-up or a complete exterior repaint, we treat every job with the same level of care and dedication.

While we're based in Hillsborough, we service the entire Auckland region and are available for travel throughout New Zealand for larger commercial projects.

Our Services

Comprehensive painting solutions for residential and commercial properties

Interior Painting

Transform your living spaces with our premium interior painting services. We handle everything from preparation to the final coat, ensuring a flawless finish that brightens your home.

Exterior Painting

Protect and beautify your property's exterior with weather-resistant paints designed for New Zealand's harsh UV conditions. We specialise in weatherboard, brick, and plaster finishes.

Roof Painting

Extend the life of your roof with our specialised roof painting and coating services. We work on tile, metal, and decramastic roofs using premium products built to last.

Commercial Projects

From offices to retail spaces, we deliver professional commercial painting with minimal disruption to your business operations. Available for projects nationwide.

Plastering & Repairs

Complete plastering services to ensure your walls are perfectly smooth before painting. We repair cracks, damage, and water stains to ensure a premium finish.

Spray Painting

Professional spray painting for doors, windows, and detailed finishes. Perfect for achieving that smooth, factory-like finish on joinery and cabinetry.
